My Favourite Pet!

Honestly, I don't know what to choose between a dog or a cat, infact i would rather pick both, but if I was brutally forced to pick one, then I just might pick a german shepherd. They're the most adorable things in the world and very intelligent, though they make tons of mess around in the first few months of their life, but I can't resist the desire to own one. On top of being an intelligent breed, german shepherds are also:

  • strong
  • courageous
  • obedient
  • excellent in focus, loyalty, tenacity
Because a german shepherd has a lot of energy, it is important that you battle it until he is satisfied by resting or relaxing. You could play fetch with him or chase him around a wide field with room for him to run at his best. Toys will come in handly at a time like night when you can't play with him due to darkness, so buying toys for him to play around with or chew should keep him busy until dawn.
